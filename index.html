School Portal is an application for people to access their school resources online, off this one app, to make it a streamlines process.

Support

As of right now we support all mainstream operating systems. This will include Windows 10/11, macOS, Arch Linux, Debian, Ubuntu, Fedora, SUSE, and more!

If you need to build it on your own, because we haven't pre-packaged an installer, use our documentation for building our Electron app with Electron-Forge.
